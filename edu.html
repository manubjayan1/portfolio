Education and Experience

Once upon a time, in the vast cosmos of my childhood dreams, I aspired to be an astronaut. I'd gaze at the stars with wonder, imagining myself navigating the celestial highways. However, reality had other plans. As school whisked me into its gravitational pull, my ambitions morphed into a love for astrophysics. It seemed like the closest I could get to those twinkling stars. So, I embarked on a journey, majoring in Physics for my Bachelor's degree and delving even deeper with a Master's in Space Science.

But life, being the cosmic jester it is, had a few tricks up its sleeve. During my Master's, I encountered a curious phenomenon: data analysis. Initially, it felt like trying to solve a complex equation in zero gravity. However, as I dug deeper, I realized something amazing - I was falling in love with data analysis, and it turned out that analyzing data was my true passion, not just reaching for the stars.

In the great cosmic comedy, I found myself navigating a different kind of space - a realm of numbers, charts, and spreadsheets. I got sucked into a black hole of data, and to my surprise, it was a thrilling ride. I couldn't resist. After finishing my Masters, I found myself in the "management nebula," working for years, balancing budgets and navigating boardrooms. Yet, the stars kept pulling me back.

Finally, I decided it was time to chase my dreams again. I pursued postgraduate diplomas in Big Data Analytics and IT Business Analysis, all the while keeping one eye on the data constellations above. Now, I'm on the verge of launching into my first job as a data analyst.
